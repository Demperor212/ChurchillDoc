How to club?

Clubs are one of the ways to make school feel more enjoyable

Usually there are a lot of clubs in your school, join the ones you are intrested. If not, create one yourself!

(The create club option is not easy especially if you are in a big school with a lot of clubs already, aka Churchill. You need genuine new idea or addressing genuine new problems or an idea teachers could not say no to.)

If you just want to have fun, be in the club, attend it, have fun!

However, if you are ambitious and want to be its executive, lead and impact the club further, you need more than that.

You need to actively participate starting from grade 10. Instead of the quiet kid who is alone is corner in the club, you need to actively engage with other members: enough that at least people know who you are and think you are a good person.

You need to be confident, at least looking so, so people know you can actually lead the club.

At last, know that a good relationship with the current executives is also important, as they are sometimes the ones to decide your fate.

Be aware that leading a club is a big time commitment, so don't become executive for 67 different clubs and have to drop 66 of them.
